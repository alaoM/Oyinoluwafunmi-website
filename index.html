On the first of January 2020, I wore an ankara Top for a new year outing I attended and took a picture. I sent this picture to my friend the stylist who made the cloth 'Temilola' and that was all I knew. I think the second day she posted the picture on her Whatsapp status to showcase her work and some minutes after, I got a message from her asking me if I was engaged because I was wearing a finger rosary. Quickly, I said nooooo (tho I was in a relationship as that time but no one had engaged me, it's my church's finger rosary abeg). Some days later, this person messages me

The said guy by 5:30am: Hello

Me by 10am: Hi

The said guy by 10pm: Hi, how are you

Me by 7am the next day: M fine

Omo, I was tired of the back and forth and odd time Hi, hello. Little did I know that this said guy works in the bank, leaves early in the morning as a Lagos Boy and comes back late in the night hence the awkward chatting time and conversation cos he barely had time. Then one night, this guy calls and says Hello, this is Victor, Temilola's friend. I was like ohh oh please can we talk later because I was busy. He says what time will I be free and I said like 10 and he was like perfect. Victor called by 10 and we talked for a long time, his airtime ran out, I called back and he was thrilled like "Aww, You called back" and I told him I was enjoying the conversation. My airtime also ran out and we had to continue the conversation on WhatsApp. That day we had our first real conversation and we talked for more than 2hours. Really from that day, it's been a roller coaster of us both with God leading us through up till this point.
